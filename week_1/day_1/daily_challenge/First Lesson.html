Welcome to the first lesson!

First, let's look at the parts of the Psychometric Exam!

Here's what you're going to face!

  • Verbal
    • a 45 minute essay
    • two 20 minute multiple choice sections
  • Quantitative
    • two 20 minute multiple choice sections
  • English Language
    • two 20 minute multiple choice sections

Remember, there will be two randomly selected pilot sections. They don't count.
However, you won't know which sections are the pilot sections while you're taking the test,
so treat every section very seriously!

Good luck!
