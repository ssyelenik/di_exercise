Conditions

Conditional statements are an universal programming paradigm to take certain actions based on data comparison. Itâ€™s our way of communicating to the computer, if something is true then perform such action, otherwise perform an other action.

See the official documentation here

Conditions are expressions that compare two elements.

Python support the following logical conditions:

  • Equals: a == b
  • Not Equals: a != b
  • Less than: a < b
  • Less than or equal to: a <= b
  • Greater than: a > b
  • Greater than or equal to: a >= b
